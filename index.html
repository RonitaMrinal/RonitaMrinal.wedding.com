How we met

In bustling Bangalore, where dreams come alive, our story began on the first day of our internship. Fate brought us together in a queue, our eyes meeting with a silent understanding. Later, in the conference room, we sat across from each other, sharing a smile that hinted at something special. After breaks and friendly lunches, our connection deepened into a friendship that felt natural. Coffee dates became our routine, filled with laughter and tech talks. Colleagues noticed the spark between us, and during Diwali Party , as we watched the sunrise together, it dawned on us that our feelings had grown beyond friendship. One ordinary evening, as he walked me home, Mrinal surprised me with a heartfelt proposal, and since then, we've been inseparable, writing our love story in the vibrant heart of Bangalore.
